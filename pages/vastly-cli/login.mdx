# vastly <em> login</em>
import { Callout } from 'nextra-theme-docs'

The `vastly login` command allows you to login to your Vastly account through [Vastly CLI](/vastly-cli). After being prompted for your email, you will receive a verification email and link to follow for authentication.

<Callout>
Keep your terminal open while waiting for your authentication email. It will timeout after 3 minutes of inactivity
</Callout>

<br/>

# Usage
<Callout type="warning">
You will not be able to login unless you've signed up for a Vastly account
</Callout>
  ```bash copy filename="terminal"
  vastly login
  ```
